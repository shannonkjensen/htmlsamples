Dentrix toolbars - overview

TOOLBARS. This page talks about toolbars.When you use this site, you will want to use the toolbars on the left side of the page. You can always find the toolbar by looking on the left side of your page. The toolbar includes 5 distinct icons: 1 Purple Unicorn, 2. Friendly Narwal, 3 Happy Bear and 4 Peter the Penguin. Select the product that you need help with in the toolbar on the left.
