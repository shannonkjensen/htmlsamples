Henry Schein One Customer Portal

If you purchase or renew a Customer Support Plan, you will have access to the Henry Schein One Customer Portal. When you login to the portal, you can:

路        Search for Knowledge Base articles, Dentrix Help topics, Dentrix Magazine articles, and blog posts simultaneously.

路        Access live, interactive webinars designed to bring users new to Dentrix up to speed quickly on products and general dental industry training topics such as understanding dental insurance plans.

路        Access to a centralized dashboard of your support cases, manage your payments, and track your staff's progress through product- and industry-based course materials.

路        Access to role-based training for clinical, front desk staff, and administrators to help onboard new staff and give existing office staff opportunities to grow their skill sets.
