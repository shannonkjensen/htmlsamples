Family File overview      

The Family File stores and displays important patient information, such as the patient's name, address, phone number, health history, birth date, insurance coverage, employer, and referral information. Patients are organized by family in the Family File. The Family File helps you to organize, store, and find patients and patient information.

The Family File toolbar

The Family File includes a toolbar to provide easy access to most of Family File's tools.

Patient Chart

Click to open the Patient Chart from which you can enter patient treatment and conditions, document and track patient care, enter periodontal treatment information, and explain and demonstrate proposed treatment to patients.

Ledger

Click to open the Dentrix Ledger from which you can enter payments, file claims, print statements, set up financial agreements, and more.

Appointment Book

Click to open the Appointment Book and schedule patients electronically and easily manage your patient appointments.

Office Manager

Click to open the Office Manager which provides several tools and reports to evaluate and improve the management processes of your practice.

eDex

Click to manage and quickly search lists of patients (active, inactive, or archived), employers, insurance carriers, labs, non-patients, providers, staff, referrals, and other contacts you may want to remember. You can also create custom contacts and contact categories to filter the Contacts List or even locate a patient's next appointment.

DXWeb

Click to display the DXWeb toolbar which works in conjunction with eCentral to provide you with the tools necessary to connect your Dentrix software to the internet.

DentriXlink

 

Click to link to supported third-party X-ray and imaging systems you've installed on your computer; if none are installed, clicking this button displays a list of third-party digital X-ray and imaging systems that you can use with Dentrix.

Quick Letters

Click to combine data from your Dentrix database with the word processing abilities of Microsoft Word to help you quickly create a letter for a single patient.

Quick Labels

Click to quickly print a variety of labels for the selected patient.

Questionnaires

Click to open the Questionnaires module from which you can make your office paperless by electronically gathering and storing the same patient information that is included on new patient forms, health history updates, and other paper forms used in your office. A red check mark in the icon indicates that the patient has filled out a questionnaire.

Patient Forms

Click to open the Dentrix Communications website from which you can create custom patient forms.

Prescriptions

Click to quickly create prescriptions and accurately track medicines prescribed to your patients. If a prescription bottle appears in the icon, the patient has a prescription.

Office Journal

Click to open the Office Journal from which you can keep a record of past events and set reminders of future events.

Document Center

Click to open the Document Center from which you can make your office paperless by scanning, capturing, and importing many types of documents, such as patient letters, EOBs (explanation of benefits), and referral letters or images (such as patient pictures and X-rays). You can attach these documents to patients, providers, insurance carriers, and referral sources. If papers appear in the lower drawer of the icon, the patient has documents saved to the Document Center.

Dentrix Marketplace
